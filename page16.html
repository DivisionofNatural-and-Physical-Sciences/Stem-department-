Biotechnology Certificate

The biotechnology program is a specialized track within the biology major to grant a 32-credit hours biotechnology certificate. These courses are offered by the Departments of Biology and Chemistry with newly offered courses for this certificate, Biotechnology and Molecular Biotechnology.

General Biology I&II, General Chemistry I&II, and Molecular Biology are requirements for the biology major. The Microbiology course and the new Biotechnology courses will fulfill the Major Elective requirements for the biology degree.
