Bioinformatics Certificate

Bridge the gap between biology and computer science with our interdisciplinary Bioinformatics Certificate program. This 18-credit collaboration between our Biology and Computer Science departments prepares students for careers at the intersection of life sciences and computational technology.

The Bioinformatics Certification program is a collaboration between the Departments of Biology and Computer Science to provide an 18-credit hour certification. The certification is available to all students and can typically be completed in two semesters. Requirements include General Biology I & II, an introductory computing course, Introduction to Data Science, and one elective from Genomics or Computational Biochemistry. Note: Computational Biochemistry requires Molecular Biology as an additional prerequisite.
