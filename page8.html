Pre-Nursing

Prepare for a rewarding career in healthcare through our Pre-Nursing program and Partnership Enrollment Program (PEP) with the University of Tennessee Health Science Center (UTHSC). Our program provides a direct pathway to nursing education and advanced healthcare degrees.

Lane College - UTHSC College of Nursing Partnership Enrollment Program (PEP)

This Partnership Enrollment Program offers full admission into the accelerated BSN or traditional BSN at UTHSC to any Lane students who fulfill the requirements of the PEP. Additionally, Lane students can be elected to participate in the bridge programs of DNP, PhD, or Dual DNP/PhD if the requirements are also met.

Requirements for Intent Enrollment at UTHSC

  • Current enrollment at Lane College
  • Good academic standing
  • Compliance with the Lane code of conduct during time of enrollment
  • Completion of UTHSC’s CON Agreement Intent to Enroll Form

Students interested in enrolling in the pre-nursing program at Lane College should complete this form: Lane College UTHSC-PEP Nursing Program Information Request

Requirements for Admission to the Traditional BSN Concentration

Completion of an associate degree in interdisciplinary studies, pre-nursing track, from Lane with a minimum overall combined 3.0 GPA (as computed by the UTHSC CON) and meeting all other requirements.

Minimum 2.6 or better for science prerequisite courses: BIO 344 Human Anatomy and Physiology I with Lab (4 credits), BIO 345 Human Anatomy and Physiology II with Lab (4 credits), and BIO 347 Microbiology with Lab (4 credits).

Requirements for Admission to the Accelerated BSN Concentration

Completion of a baccalaureate degree from Lane with a minimum overall combined 3.0 GPA (as computed by the UTHSC CON) and meeting all other requirements.

Minimum 2.6 or better for science prerequisite courses: BIO 344, BIO 345, and BIO 347 (each with labs, 4 credits).

Students with any bachelor’s degree from Lane College are eligible, but they must complete the prerequisite courses. The Pre-nursing track in the Biology major includes all prerequisite and recommended courses.

More information about the Partnership Enrollment Program is available here: Nursing Partnership Enrollment Program at University of Tennessee Health Science Center

Associate Degree of Science — Nursing Track (for Direct Admission to UTHSC BSN)

General Education Requirements:

  • First-year and Second Year Seminars — 5 courses (5 credits): ORN 110, LCC 112, LCC 120, CPI 101, LCC 210
  • Language Arts/Communication — 2 courses (6 hours): ENG 133, COM 230
  • Social & Behavioral Science — 2 courses (6 hours): SOC 131, PSY 130
  • Humanities & Fine Arts — 2 courses (6 hours): ENG 200-level literature option + one ART/MUS/REL/PHL choice
  • Mathematics — 2 courses (6 hours): MAT 133, MAT 233
  • Physical & Life Science — 2 courses (8 hours): BIO 141, BIO 142
  • Foreign Language — 1 course (3 credits): FRE 130 or SPN 130
  • Health & Fitness — 1 course (2 credits): PER 222 or PER 225
